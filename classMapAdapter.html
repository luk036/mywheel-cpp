* MapAdapter Structure:
*
* Original Container: [A] [B] [C] [D] [E]
*                    [0] [1] [2] [3] [4]  <-- Indices become keys
*
* MapAdapter Interface:
*
* operator[](0) → A
* operator[](1) → B
* operator[](2) → C
* operator[](3) → D
* operator[](4) → E
*
* begin() → iterator to (0, A)
* end()   → iterator past (4, E)
*
* enumerate() → [(0, A), (1, B), (2, C), (3, D), (4, E)]
*
